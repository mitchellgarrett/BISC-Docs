## Logical Instructions

### AND - Bitwise And
\tformat{0x1A}

#### Desciption:
The `AND` performs a bitwise and operation on all of the bits in the two source registers and stores the result in the destination register.

#### Action:
~~~
rd := rs $\land$ rs1
~~~

#### Assembler Syntax:
~~~
AND {rd}, {rs0}, {rs1}
~~~

#### Example:
~~~
AND r0, r1, r2
~~~

### BSL - Bit Shift Left
\tformat{0x1D}

#### Desciption:
The `BSL` instruction shifts the value of `rs~0~` left by `rs~`~` bits.

#### Action:
~~~
rd := rs << rs1
~~~

#### Assembler Syntax:
~~~
BSL {rd}, {rs0}, {rs1}
~~~

#### Example:
~~~
BSL r0, r1, r2
~~~

### BSR - Bit Shift Right
\tformat{0x1E}

#### Desciption:
The `BSR` instruction shifts the value of `rs~0~` right by `rs~`~` bits.

#### Action:
~~~
rd := rs0 >> rs1
~~~

#### Assembler Syntax:
~~~
BSR {rd}, {rs0}, {rs1}
~~~

#### Example:
~~~

~~~
